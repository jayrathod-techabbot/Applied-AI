# Quiz: AI Compliance

## Questions

### Question 1
What is the EU AI Act?

A) A database of AI systems
B) Comprehensive EU AI regulation
C) An AI testing framework
D) An open source license

### Question 2
Which AI applications are prohibited under EU AI Act?

A) All chatbots
B) Unacceptable risk applications
C) Spam filters
D) Recommender systems

### Question 3
What does GDPR require for automated decisions?

A) No restrictions
B) Human review option
C) Complete prohibition
D) Special license only

### Question 4
What is a DPIA?

A) Data Privacy Impact Assessment
B) Digital Product Integration Algorithm
C) Data Protection in AI
D) Digital Privacy Information Act

### Question 5
What is the maximum fine under GDPR?

A) €1 million
B) €10 million
C) €20 million or 4% of revenue
D) €100 million

### Question 6
Which is required for high-risk AI under EU AI Act?

A) No requirements
B) Risk assessment only
C) Full documentation and oversight
D) Just a disclaimer

### Question 7
What does "privacy by design" mean?

A) Design for looks
B) Build privacy into systems from start
C) Add privacy later
D) Use privacy tools only

### Question 8
What is a data subject right?

A) Right to be forgotten
B) Right to use data freely
C) Right to sell data
D) Right to ignore privacy

## Answers

1. B - Comprehensive EU AI regulation
2. B - Unacceptable risk applications
3. B - Human review option
4. A - Data Privacy Impact Assessment
5. C - €20 million or 4% of revenue
6. C - Full documentation and oversight
7. B - Build privacy into systems from start
8. A - Right to be forgotten
