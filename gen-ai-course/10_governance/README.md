# Module 10: AI Governance

This module covers AI governance, risk management, responsible AI practices, and compliance frameworks.

## Topics

1. **[01_risks_guardrails/](01_risks_guardrails/)** - AI Risks & Guardrails
   - Identifying AI risks
   - Implementing safety guardrails
   - Risk mitigation strategies

2. **[02_responsible_ai/](02_responsible_ai/)** - Responsible AI
   - Ethical AI principles
   - Fairness and bias mitigation
   - Transparency and accountability

3. **[03_compliance/](03_compliance/)** - AI Compliance
   - Regulatory frameworks
   - GDPR and AI
   - Industry standards

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:
- Understand AI-related risks and how to mitigate them
- Implement responsible AI practices
- Navigate regulatory compliance requirements

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of AI/ML fundamentals
- Knowledge of AI ethics concepts
- Awareness of regulatory requirements
