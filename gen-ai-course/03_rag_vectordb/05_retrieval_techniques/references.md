# Retrieval Techniques - References

## Research Papers

1. **HyDE: Hypothetical Document Embeddings** (2022)
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.10496
   - Technique for improving zero-shot retrieval

2. **Maximal Marginal Relevance** (1998)
   - https://www.cs.cmu.edu/afs/cs.cmu.edu/project/learn-20/lib/photoz/.g/.gtk/users/cbahl/plateforme/html/media/p119-carbonell.pdf
   - Original MMR paper by Carbonell and Goldstein

3. **Dense Passage Retrieval** (2020)
   - https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.09541
   - DPR for open-domain question answering
