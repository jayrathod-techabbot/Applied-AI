## Refund Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with our service. If you are not, we offer the following refund options:

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
All new subscriptions (Starter and Professional plans) are eligible for a full refund within **30 days of the initial purchase date**. This applies to:
- First-time subscribers only
- Monthly plan purchases
- Annual plan purchases (prorated if used for more than 30 days)

**How to request a refund:**
1. Log in to your account at app.support-platform.com.
2. Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Refund Request**.
3. Select the charge you want refunded and provide a brief reason.
4. Submit the form. Our billing team reviews all requests within 2 business days.
5. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within **5–10 business days**.

### Exceptions — Non-Refundable Items
The following are not eligible for refunds:
- Add-on purchases (additional storage, extra API call packs)
- Subscriptions that have been active for more than 30 days
- Enterprise plan custom development work
- Charges that resulted from excessive API usage beyond plan limits

### Prorated Credits for Downgrades
If you downgrade your plan mid-billing-cycle, we issue a **prorated credit** to your account for the unused portion of the higher-tier plan. Credits appear on your next invoice automatically.

## Invoice Questions

### How to Download Your Invoice
1. Log in to your account.
2. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.
3. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.
4. Click **Download PDF** next to any invoice.

Invoices are also automatically emailed to your billing contact email address on each billing date.

### Invoice Details
Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number and date
- Billing period
- Plan name and unit price
- Number of seats billed
- Any applicable discounts or credits
- Total amount charged
- Payment method used (last 4 digits of card)
- Company VAT/GST number (if provided in your billing settings)

### Updating Billing Information on Invoices
To add your company name, address, or VAT number to future invoices:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Billing Information**.
2. Fill in your company name, billing address, and tax ID.
3. Click **Save**. Changes apply to all future invoices.

**Note:** We cannot retroactively modify already-issued invoices. If you need a corrected invoice for accounting purposes, contact billing@support-platform.com with your invoice number and the corrections needed.

## Subscription Management

### How to Upgrade Your Plan
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Plan**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Select the new plan and review the prorated charge summary.
4. Confirm the upgrade. The new plan takes effect immediately.
5. You are charged the prorated difference for the remaining days in your current billing cycle.

### How to Downgrade Your Plan
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Plan**.
2. Click **Change Plan** and select a lower-tier plan.
3. The downgrade takes effect at the **end of your current billing cycle** to avoid disruption.
4. A prorated credit is applied to your next invoice.

### How to Cancel Your Subscription
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Subscription**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. You will be asked to confirm and optionally provide a cancellation reason (helps us improve).
4. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access until then.
5. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted unless you re-subscribe.

### How to Pause Your Subscription
Pausing is available on Professional and Enterprise plans only.
- You can pause your subscription for up to **3 months**.
- During the pause, your account is in read-only mode (no API calls, no uploads).
- Billing is suspended during the pause period.
- To pause: **Settings → Billing → Subscription → Pause Subscription**.

### Annual Plan Renewal
Annual subscriptions auto-renew 7 days before the anniversary date. You will receive:
- A renewal reminder email 30 days before renewal.
- A final reminder 7 days before renewal.
- A receipt email immediately after the charge.

To cancel renewal, do so at least 48 hours before the renewal date.

## Common Billing Issues

### Q: I was charged twice for the same billing period.
**A:** This can happen if a payment fails initially and our system retries. Please check your bank statement to confirm whether both charges settled or if one was a hold/authorization. If you were genuinely double-charged, contact billing@support-platform.com with your invoice numbers. We will verify and refund any duplicate within 3 business days.

### Q: My payment failed. What should I do?
**A:** Payments fail for several reasons: insufficient funds, expired card, bank fraud protection, or billing address mismatch. When a payment fails:
1. You receive an email notification immediately.
2. We retry the payment after 3 days, then again after 7 days.
3. If the payment still fails after 3 attempts, your account is downgraded to a limited free tier.

To resolve: Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods**, update or re-enter your card details, then click **Retry Payment**.

### Q: I see an unexpected charge on my statement.
**A:** Search for "SP Technologies" on your statement (our billing descriptor). If you still do not recognize it, it may be a charge for additional API call packs or a seat expansion that someone on your team authorized. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoice History** to review all charges. If you believe a charge is unauthorized, contact billing@support-platform.com immediately.

### Q: Can I get a tax exemption applied to my account?
**A:** Yes. If your organization is tax-exempt (e.g., non-profit, government), submit your tax exemption certificate to billing@support-platform.com. We will apply the exemption to future invoices and issue a credit for any taxes charged in the current billing period.

### Q: I cancelled but was still charged.
**A:** Cancellations take effect at the **end** of the current billing period — not immediately. If you cancelled after your renewal date, the charge for the new period applies. If you believe this is an error, provide your cancellation confirmation email to billing@support-platform.com and we will review it within 1 business day.
