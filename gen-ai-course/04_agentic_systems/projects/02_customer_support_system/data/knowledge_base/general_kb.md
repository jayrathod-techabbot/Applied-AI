## Service Availability and SLA

### Uptime Guarantee
We guarantee **99.9% uptime** for all plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise), measured monthly excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

| Plan         | Uptime SLA | Response SLA (critical)  |
|--------------|------------|--------------------------|
| Starter      | 99.9%      | Best effort              |
| Professional | 99.9%      | 8 business hours         |
| Enterprise   | 99.95%     | 1 hour (24/7)            |

### Scheduled Maintenance
Maintenance windows are scheduled during low-traffic periods:
- **Primary window:** Sundays, 2:00 AM – 4:00 AM EST
- Customers receive email notification at least 72 hours in advance.
- Maintenance status is posted at status.support-platform.com.

### Incident Communication
In the event of an unplanned outage:
1. Status page (status.support-platform.com) is updated within 15 minutes of incident detection.
2. Customers on Professional and Enterprise plans receive email notifications.
3. Post-incident report (root cause analysis) is published within 72 hours.

### Service Credits
If uptime falls below the guaranteed SLA, you are eligible for service credits:
- 99.0–99.9%: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 95.0–99.0%: 25% credit of monthly fee
- Below 95.0%: 50% credit of monthly fee

To claim a credit, email billing@support-platform.com within 30 days of the incident with the ticket or incident reference number.

## Privacy and Data Policy

### What Data We Collect
We collect the following data when you use our platform:
- **Account information:** Name, email address, company name, billing address.
- **Usage data:** API call logs, session records, feature usage statistics.
- **Support tickets:** Messages you submit to our support system.
- **Payment information:** Processed by Stripe; we store only the last 4 digits and expiry.

### How We Use Your Data
- To provide, operate, and improve our services.
- To send transactional emails (invoices, receipts, alerts).
- To send product updates and newsletters (opt-in only — unsubscribe anytime).
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties.
- We do NOT use your support ticket content for LLM training without explicit consent.

### Data Retention
- Active account data: Retained for the duration of the subscription plus 30 days after cancellation.
- Audit logs: 1 year for Professional, 7 years for Enterprise (regulatory compliance).
- Payment records: 7 years (legal requirement).
- After retention period: Data is irreversibly deleted from all storage systems.

### GDPR and Data Rights (EU Customers)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data.
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Objection:** Object to certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@support-platform.com. We respond within 30 days.

### CCPA (California Residents)
California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To learn more, see our full Privacy Policy at support-platform.com/privacy.

## Account Management

### Creating an Account
1. Go to support-platform.com and click **Get Started Free**.
2. Enter your work email and create a password.
3. Verify your email address (check your inbox for a verification link).
4. Complete your organization profile.
5. Choose a plan or start your 14-day free trial.

### Managing Team Members
- **Add a user:** **Settings → Team → Invite Member** → Enter email and assign role.
- **Roles available:** Owner, Admin, Agent, Viewer.
- **Remove a user:** **Settings → Team** → Click the three-dot menu next to the user → **Remove**.
- Removed users immediately lose access. Their historical activity is retained in audit logs.

### Password and Security
- To reset your password: Click **Forgot Password** on the login page. A reset link is emailed within 2 minutes.
- To change your password while logged in: **Settings → Security → Change Password**.
- We require passwords to be at least 12 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
2FA is strongly recommended and can be enforced org-wide by Admins.
- **Setup:** **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication → Enable**.
- Supported methods: Authenticator app (TOTP), SMS (US numbers only), hardware keys (YubiKey).
- If you lose access to your 2FA device, contact support@support-platform.com with your account email and a government-issued ID for identity verification.

### Transferring Account Ownership
Only the current Owner can transfer ownership:
1. **Settings → Team** → Find the new owner and click **Promote to Owner**.
2. Confirm the transfer. The previous owner's role becomes Admin.

## Security Practices

### Encryption
- All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
- All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 on AWS S3 and RDS.

### Infrastructure Security
- Hosted on AWS with multi-region redundancy.
- Automated vulnerability scanning runs daily.
- Third-party penetration testing conducted annually.
- SOC 2 Type II audit report available upon request (Enterprise customers).

### Reporting a Security Vulnerability
We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly:
- **Email:** security@support-platform.com (PGP key available on our website)
- Do NOT publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had 90 days to address it.
- We offer a bug bounty program — see support-platform.com/security/bugbounty for details.
