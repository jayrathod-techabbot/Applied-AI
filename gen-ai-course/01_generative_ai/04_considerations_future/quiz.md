## Questions

### Question 7
What is a "jailbreak" in AI context?

A) A prison software
B) Attempting to bypass AI safety measures
C) Model training technique
D) Data storage method

### Question 8
What is an emerging trend in generative AI?

A) Decreasing model sizes
B) Less multimodal capability
C) More agentic AI systems

## Answers

1. B - Hallucinations are false but plausible information
2. B - Bias or discriminatory content
3. B - Systematic errors favoring certain groups
4. B - Limited context windows
5. B - Users deserve to know when interacting with AI
6. B - Handles multiple data types
7. B - Bypassing safety measures
8. C - More agentic AI systems
